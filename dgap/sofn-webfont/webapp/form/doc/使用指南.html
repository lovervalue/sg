使用指南

在 form 上添加 name属性标识验证的表单;

为 form 标签指定 novidate，禁用浏览器默认验证机制;

为 input 标签指定验证规则;

<form name="myForm" novalidate>
    <div class="form-item form-group">
        <label class="title required">必填</label>
        <input type="text" class="gui-input" ng-model="required" name="requiredField" required placeholder="必填" />
    </div>
    <div class="form-item form-group">
        <label class="title">长度</label>
        <input type="text" class="gui-input" ng-model="min" name="minField" minlength="5" maxlength="20" required 
            placeholder="请输入5-20位字符" />
    </div>
    <div class="form-item form-group">
        <label class="title">正则模式</label>
        <input type="text" class="gui-input" ng-model="pattern" name="patternField" ng-pattern="/^[a-zA-Z]{1,20}$/" 
            required placeholder="1-20位英文字符" />
    </div>
    <div class="text-center">
        <button type="submit" class="gui-btn" ng-disabled="myForm.$invalid" disabled="disabled">提交</button>
    </div>
</form>
